Roof of the World

This is the promo of a documentary about Tibet and Tibetan people. We traveled more than 20 days in Tibet, got 10 tapes of footage. Film with a DVCAM camera, it was my graduation trip and my first personal project.

Special thanks to Ding Siyue, who sponsored the trip in summer 2012. Filmed and Edited by CY Xu.
